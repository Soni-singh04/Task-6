HISTORY OF VAISHALI DISTRICT

Vaishali district, located in the state of Bihar, India, has a rich historical and cultural significance. It is known as one of the most ancient places in India and holds a prominent place in the history of Buddhism and Jainism. Medieval History:During the Gupta dynasty, Vaishali continued to flourish as a center of culture and trade.It later came under the rule of the Maurya dynasty during the reign of Chandragupta Maurya and Ashoka the Great. Modern History:In British India, Vaishali was part of the Tirhut division.After India's independence in 1947, Vaishali became a part of Bihar and was officially declared a district in 1972.
